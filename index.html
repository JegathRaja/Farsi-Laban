Who We Are

Our Story

Farsi Laban was born from a deep love for the rich dessert culture of Dubai. We believed that the world deserved to taste the magic of authentic Laban — creamy, layered, and crafted with tradition.

Every spoonful is a journey through the vibrant streets of old Dubai. Our recipes are passed down through generations, perfected with the finest pistachios, Belgian chocolates, and the creamiest dairy.
